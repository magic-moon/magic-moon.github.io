The Boy, the Mole, the Fox and the Horse (2022)

Zhongkui Zhang / 2023-03-19

“The Boy, the Mole, the Fox and the Horse” is a book written and illustrated by Charlie Mackesy. The book was first published in 2019 and quickly became a bestseller, touching the hearts of readers around the world with its messages of kindness, empathy, and love.

The story follows the journey of a boy who meets a mole, a fox, and a horse, each of whom has their own unique perspective on life. Together, they explore themes of friendship, courage, vulnerability, and the importance of being kind to oneself and others.

The book is a beautifully illustrated work of art, with hand-drawn illustrations that complement the heartfelt and uplifting messages in the text.
